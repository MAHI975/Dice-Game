Game Rules:

  1. There are two players in this game, who play in turns
  2. In each turn the player can roll two dices any number of times they wish and each time the number of the dice is added to his CURRENT SCORE
  3. But if the player rolls a 1 in any of his dices his CURRENT SCORE gets lost and its the next player's turn. Again if anyone rolls two 6 all of his GLOBAL SCORE gets lost
  4. However a player can choose to hold his CURRENT SCORE any time which makes the CURRENT SCORE get added to the GLOBAL SCORE
  5. The first player's GLOBAL SCORE to reach 100 or the score given by the players is the Winner!(GOOD LUCK!!!)
